About

My work focuses on connecting deep learning, geometric vision, and robotic perception for medical and surgical applications. I am especially interested in structured visual representations that support robust localization, reconstruction, and tool understanding in challenging surgical environments.

Research Interests

Surgical video understanding, medical image analysis, keypoint detection, segmentation-guided refinement, monocular 3D reconstruction, and robotics perception.
