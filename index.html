Mask-Guided Multi-Frame Surgical Tool Keypoint Localization

Surgical Computer Vision Keypoint Localization SAM2 Heatmap Regression Temporal Modeling
Available

Developed a multi-frame surgical tool keypoint localization pipeline for endoscopic videos, using heatmap-based landmark prediction, SAM2-derived mask priors, and segmentation-guided refinement to improve tool tip and anchor localization under occlusion, specularity, and motion.

Method

  • Curated multi-frame surgical video clips with action-dependent tip and anchor annotations.
  • Trained heatmap-based models for surgical tool landmark localization on the center frame.
  • Generated SAM2-derived tool masks as structural priors for segmentation-guided refinement.
  • Evaluated action-wise and landmark-wise performance under clipping, grasping, and dissecting actions.

Outputs

  • Quantitative evaluation using precision, recall, F1 score, and L2 localization distance.
  • Action-wise comparison across clipping, grasping, and dissecting surgical tool actions.
  • Ablation study comparing coarse heatmap prediction, refinement, mask prior, and auxiliary supervision.
  • Qualitative visualization of heatmaps, mask priors, refined predictions, and failure cases.

Monocular 3D Human Pose and Metric Depth Estimation

3D Vision YOLOv8-Pose Depth Anything V2 LiDAR Validation ARKit Intrinsics
Available

Built a monocular 3D reconstruction pipeline that combines 2D human pose estimation, foundation model depth prediction, camera intrinsic calibration, and LiDAR based validation for metric 3D pose and depth estimation.

Pipeline

  • YOLOv8-Pose for 2D human keypoint detection.
  • Depth Anything V2 for monocular depth estimation.
  • iPhone ARKit camera intrinsics for geometric projection.
  • RGB-to-3D keypoint reconstruction using depth and camera calibration.

Evaluation and Visualization

  • LiDAR-based validation for metric depth consistency.
  • 3D reconstruction visualization of human pose.
  • Short demo video or GIF for real-time reconstruction.
  • Quantitative and qualitative comparison of estimated depth.
